🧠 Knowledge

📘 KWLG-CNCPT – Conceptual Knowledge
📖 Understanding of concepts, theories, and principles.
💡 Explain key ideas in calculus including limits and differentiation.

🛠 KWLG-PRCDR – Procedural Knowledge
📖 Use of established methods and techniques in a field.
💡 Use integration by parts to evaluate integrals.

🔗 KWLG-LINK – Interdisciplinary Linkages
📖 Make meaningful connections across disciplines.
💡 Link mathematical modeling to environmental predictions.

⚙️ Skills

🧰 SKILL-TASK – Task Execution
📖 Perform technical or cognitive tasks.
💡 Build a web application from specifications.

🧠 SKILL-ANALYZE – Analytical Thinking
📖 Analyze and synthesize complex information.
💡 Compare two health interventions based on data.

🧪 SKILL-EVALUATE – Method Selection & Evaluation
📖 Select and assess methods or tools appropriately.
💡 Choose a statistical test that fits your hypothesis.

🧩 Application

📎 APPL-KNOW – Apply Knowledge
📖 Apply theoretical understanding in real contexts.
💡 Use climate data to inform sustainable farming strategies.

🛠 APPL-SKILL – Apply Skills
📖 Use hands-on or digital tools to create/solve.
💡 Design a prototype using CAD software.

🌍 Values & Ethics

⚖️ ETHIC-PRACTICE-ETHIC – Ethical Practice
📖 Act with honesty, fairness, and academic integrity.
💡 Cite all sources in your term paper.

🎯 EMP-OWN – Responsibility
📖 Take ownership of tasks and results.
💡 Submit quality work on time without reminders.

🤝 EMP-GROUP – Teamwork
📖 Work collaboratively and communicate well in teams.
💡 Lead a study group and divide work fairly.
